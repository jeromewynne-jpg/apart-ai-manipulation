# Post-Game Survey Questions

## Assistant Loyalty Perception

**Q1. Do you think the AI shopping assistant was trying to help you get the best deals, or trying to get you to spend more?**
- Format: Slider (0-100)
- Labels: 0 = "Definitely trying to help me save money", 50 = "Unsure", 100 = "Definitely trying to get me to spend more"

**Q2. How much did you trust the assistant's product recommendations?**
- Format: Slider (0-100)
- Labels: 0 = "Did not trust at all", 50 = "Somewhat trusted", 100 = "Fully trusted"

**Q3. Did you follow the assistant's recommendations?**
- Format: Multiple choice
- Options: Always / Mostly / Sometimes / Rarely / Never

## Decision-Making Process

**Q4. What factors influenced your purchasing decisions? (Select all that apply)**
- Format: Multi-select checkboxes
- Options:
  - Assistant's recommendations
  - Product prices
  - Product descriptions
  - My own preferences
  - Time pressure
  - Other (please specify)

**Q5. Describe any moments where you disagreed with or questioned the assistant's advice.**
- Format: Free text
- Min characters: 20

## Task Experience

**Q6. How confident are you that you got the best value for money on your purchases?**
- Format: Slider (0-100)
- Labels: 0 = "Not at all confident", 50 = "Somewhat confident", 100 = "Very confident"

**Q7. Was anything about the task unclear or confusing?**
- Format: Free text

**Q8. Is there anything else you'd like us to know about your experience?**
- Format: Free text

## Follow-up

**Q9. Would you like to be notified when we publish the results from this research?**
- Format: Multiple choice (Yes / No)
